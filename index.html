CureFest 2024

Help Find A Cure For Lupus

CureFest is an inspiring festival committed to raising awareness about Lupus and supporting the crucial work of the Lupus Research Alliance. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Golden Gate Park in San Francisco, this three-day event, scheduled from May 8th to May 10th, is a celebration of unity, hope, and the collective effort to find a cure for Lupus. You can expect a vibrant atmosphere filled with live music from your favorite artists, engaging speakers, and interactive exhibits designed to educate and foster a sense of community. All proceeds generated during CureFest are dedicated to funding Lupus research initiatives, bringing us one step closer to unlocking the mysteries of this complex autoimmune disease. It's not just a festival; it's a heartfelt movement dedicated to making a tangible impact in the quest for a cure to Lupus. Buy your tickets now!
